    • Topic Overview

    Do you ever wonder about your life and the condition that you will be in your future? What if there was an application to record your habits and provide you data on what health or disease you will have? Well, you can learn about you're projected health based on others like you. Our habits lead to our health and our actions will pay off. This developing site will provide you tips and data based on your lifestyle that you can visualize and see how it sounds to you. With our help, you can make life changes to improve your life to live long and healthy.

    With some input from you, we are here to give you suggestions that you can apply. The most important thing in life is not wealth but health. We want to provide inspirational reads to help you with physical and mental needs. Enjoy every day and the better things to come.

    Potential Apps

    • Physical Health: The first app will provide tips onto how to improve and optimize the potential of your body. People will find it useful to input their usual labor schedule and leisure habbits to analyze their current condition(s). The purpose is to provide multiple solutions to improve their body's function and well-being.

    • Mental Health: The second app will be a platform to connect will people near and far to share dialog and improve mental health. This social network shares popular stories and questions that the user can be involved in. You will be able to hear and give input on real life topics affecting our lives today. A place to discuss trying matters or to pass on an uplifting message.
